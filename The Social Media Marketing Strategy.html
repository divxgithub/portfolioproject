In the scenario of the market where every FMCG product one wants to buy is available in multiple different websites at little to the same price of the product makes it hard to sell any consumer goods with trust, in this competitive environment making yourself or your brand shine and trustworthy differently is key to successful venture. Currently in the world social media has the upper hand in comparison to the TV commercials.

The steps to grow in social media is called social media marketing, the word social media means the platform to showcase, the social media marketing uses the social network to market the product or to engage with the new and existing customer.

The Marketing consist of three type of ‘P’ these p in marketing can really help in making a product or a brand successful in the market P’s in marketing

Product

The product means what one company sells. It could be a physical product, it could be a digital service, it could be offline or online consulting. While Selling The product to the users wants to expect the best experience so make sure to include good design, the best quality, good packaging, and the best features it should be delivered with..

Price

The price means how much does the product or service actually cost, is it really value for money or value of time for the customer who is going to use it. Price Of the product or service can be determined in three different types Higher Price: Price Of the product is higher it is considered to be a high quality create the impression of the luxury products. Similar To Competitors: Price Of the product is similar to the other alternative or competition to the market then the product or service is chosen based on the better and quantity of the features present.
